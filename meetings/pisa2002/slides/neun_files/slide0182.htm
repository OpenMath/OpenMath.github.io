What is Math-Net?
• Math-Net is the idea of a new information and
   communication system in mathematics built up by a
   common effort of the  mathematical community.
•
• The mathematical community (individuals, mathematical
   institutions, all other institutions which provide relevant
   information  for mathematics) is organized in the Math-Net
   Initiative.
