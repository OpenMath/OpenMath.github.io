Exercises

  • ActiveMath also allows exercises
  • An interactive exercise has three parts:
    • A graphical user-interface
    • A mathematical content
    • Connectivity
  • Content: a mathematical system's language
  • GUI: no installation, immediate start
  • they should run together (connectivity)
  • user-model must be informed of the result
