Math and E-Learning
Computer Algebra System supports the didactical approach based on descriptive, intuitive and experimental aspects.
WWW and the e-learning explosion impose to transport  the new contents and didactical approach on the net.
Web Mathematics Exercise
It should:
present a user friendly  interface
follow the student in the learning process by being decomposed in incremental steps
allow the learner to complete the mathematic expression easily
MoMAM@th
MoMAMath is an on-line interactive software for mathematics learning.
At the moment it is in Italian and it is accessible through the following web site www.momanet.it/momamath.
