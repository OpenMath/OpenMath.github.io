Building a web mathematic exercise that enables the previous didactic approach, does not mean to implement simple right or wrong answers, but a more complex application that follows the student in the learning process by being decomposed in incremental steps.
Our experience in e-learning application suggests that in order to produce good quality e-learning mathematics exercises it is important to provide user-friendly tools to the student. The user has to pay attention to the content rather than the way s/he has to write mathematical expressions.
