Web Services Overview
§ Web Service technologies allow:
§ Integration of computational components
§ Distribution of components (useful for pervasive computing /
collaborative working / sharing hardware …)
§ Service providers / IP stakeholders to control exactly what they
expose to clients
§ Key Technologies (WorldWide Web Consortium, …):
§ SOAP (XML-messaging)
§ WSDL (Low-level interface description)
§ Choreography languages (BPEL4WS, WSCI, …)
§ Implementations:
§ IBM Websphere, Microsoft .NET, BEA WebLogic, Sun JWSDP,
TheMindElectric Glue, …
