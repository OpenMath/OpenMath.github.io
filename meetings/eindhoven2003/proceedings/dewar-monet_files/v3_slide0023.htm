Why Mathematical Web Services?
§ Mathematics underpins a large proportion of
scientific and commercial activities
§ NAG software used in Engineering, Manufacturing,
Finance, Utility Provision, Astrophysics, Education, …
§ Software increasingly complex and specialised
§ different languages for application and algorithm
§ application developers lack mathematical knowledge to
identify most appropriate technologies
§ Mathematics is relatively well-defined so
automating service discovery and choreography
should be feasible
