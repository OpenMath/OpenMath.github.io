Origins of Categorial Semantics

  • Origins in formal syntax and semantics research fields of linguistics and logic

  • Originally a calculus of syntactic categories

    • np • ( np / s \ vp) • vp --> s ``A noun phrase followed by (a sentence missing a noun phrase on the left and a verb phrase on the right) followed by a verb phrase, makes a sentence”

  • Via compositionality principle a calculus of semantic types

    • Semantic algebra homomorphic to syntactic algebra
    • N • ( N / S \ V) • V --> S
