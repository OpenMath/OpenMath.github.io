Organising CDs

  • CDs contain groups of related symbols

    • e.g. transc1 contains transcendental functions

  • Related CDs collected into CD Groups

    • e.g. Linear Algebra CD Group
      • linalg1: simple operations such as determinant
      • linalg2: row-oriented constructors
      • linalg3: column-oriented constructors
      • linalg4: advanced operations such as eigenvalue
      • linalg5: constructors for structured matrices
