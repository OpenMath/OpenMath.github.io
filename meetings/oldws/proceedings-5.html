Proceedings of OpenMath Workshop 5

Bath, 12-13 January 1996

Workshop 5 took place at the University of Bath. At this workshop there were relatively few presentations; most of the time was spent in discussions or in small working groups. There was also a "private" OpenMath meeting during the two days prior to the workshop. A handful of notes and slides from this workshop are available, and mini-reports from the working groups should appear soon.
