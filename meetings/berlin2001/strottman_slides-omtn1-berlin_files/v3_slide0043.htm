Type vs. Range
v Extensional notation is ambiguous
– Range of quantification vs. type of variable
– OpenMath resolved ambiguity in favor of
“range” interpretation in definite integration
v Generalized quantifiers will need none, one
OR both of type/range in addition to “body”
– None:  bind(q,v,e);        Type: bind(q,v:t,e)
– Range: apply(q,lv.e,r);  Both: apply(q,lv:t.e,r)
