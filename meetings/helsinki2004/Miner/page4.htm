The LaTeX Camp

LaTeX workflows are favored by society publishers of mathematics and theoretical physics research literature.

  • Tightly-coupled to a skilled and devoted author community
  • Their customers are mainly interested in electronic delivery of print documents, specifically peer-reviewed articles, typically via PDF.
  • Traditional scholarly apparatus of citations, etc. is important.
  • There is some interest in use of XML, especially for information management, e.g. blibliographic metadata.
  • Journal costs are a major issue.
